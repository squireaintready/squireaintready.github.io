About

02 — The engineer

SJ

I'm a software and AI engineer based in New York. I like building things people actually use — and shipping them all the way to production, not just to a demo.

My work spans the whole stack: type-safe frontends, authoritative real-time game servers, large civic-data pipelines, and LLM features engineered to stay grounded in real data. I care about the parts that never show up in a screenshot — test coverage, offline behavior, accessibility, and interfaces that read like they were typeset, not assembled.

Lately that has meant a prediction-market newsroom, a property-intelligence platform serving millions of parcels, a real-time 3D poker table, and a tip calculator a restaurant runs on every night.
