01

The report

phos-analysis is an interactive equity-research report on First Phosphate Corp. (CSE: PHOS / OTCQX: FRSPF / FSE: KD0) — the only pure-play LFP-battery phosphate developer on a North-American exchange. It reads the company straight from its filings: the project, the market, the balance sheet, the peers, and what the price implies.

The whole thing is one long, navigable document — ten sections from snapshot and thesis through project, market, supply chain, financials, capital, valuation, management and ESG — with a live coverage summary pinned up top.

02

Highlights

01

Live coverage summary

Share price (15-minute delayed), a bear-to-bull fair-value range, market cap, market-cap-to-NAV, PEA NPV and IRR, and cash runway — the whole picture at a glance.

02

Straight from the filings

Every figure is sourced from SEDAR+ regulatory filings through Q3 FY2026, not press releases — so the numbers trace back to a primary document.

03

Built like a product

A ⌘K command palette, section navigation, the same modern type system, and a fully responsive layout — a research report that behaves like software.

04

Honest by design

Clearly labeled research & education only — not investment advice, with every data vintage shown.

03

How it's built

A vanilla-JavaScript front end pulls delayed market data, renders the fair-value range and financial tables as lightweight data-viz, and deploys on Vercel. No framework — just the data, the filings, and a clean reading experience.
